Sunday Red E.

Sunday Red E. (Ready) is my capstone project where I utilized the Data Science methods I have learned in my bootcamp in order to apply them to a personal interest of mine in the game of golf.

The name Sunday Red comes from Tiger Woods, one of the game's greatest players, with 15 Major Championships, 82 career PGA Tour wins, and is known for his signature Red and Black look come Sunday and is the reason for the name of my project.

P.S. The E stands for "Engineered"
